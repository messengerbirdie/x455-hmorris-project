About Sally

Three Columns with Expandable Text

Personal

Born and raised in Sacramento, Sally always had a passion for helping others, which led them to pursue a career in nursing. After completing nursing school, they dedicated several years to providing compassionate care in various healthcare settings. However, over time, Sally realized that their love for assisting people extended beyond just medical care. This led to a shift in their career, where they transitioned into the world of aesthetics.

With a deep desire to make a meaningful impact, they moved to the Sierras, a region known for its natural beauty but limited access to specialized beauty services. Here, Sally combined their medical background with their newfound love for skincare and wellness, offering personalized aesthetician services to a community that often struggled to find such care. With a commitment to enhancing both physical and mental well-being, Sally brings a holistic approach to every treatment, creating an environment where clients feel rejuvenated, relaxed, and truly cared for.

Education

Sally’s educational journey began at Greenfield High School in Sacramento, where she excelled in science and health courses, igniting her passion for caring for others. After graduation, she pursued a Bachelor of Science in Nursing at Riverstone University, a well-regarded school known for its hands-on approach to healthcare.

At Sierra Valley, Sally learned advanced techniques in skincare, facials, and body treatments, blending her nursing knowledge with aesthetic practices. With both nursing and aesthetician certifications, she transitioned her career to offering specialized services in the Sierras, where her unique skill set is helping people feel both physically and emotionally rejuvenated.

Specialties

While studying, she also completed volunteer work at local clinics, further strengthening her desire to help those in need. Upon earning her nursing degree, Sally worked as a registered nurse at Sacramento General Hospital for several years. However, her interest in holistic wellness led her to enroll in the Sierra Valley School of Aesthetics, where she completed an intensive program in medical aesthetics.

Sally specializes in a variety of aesthetic treatments, combining her medical background with advanced skincare techniques to provide personalized care. Her specialties include rejuvenating facials, acne treatments, anti-aging therapies, and custom skincare regimens tailored to each client’s needs. Sally is particularly skilled in microdermabrasion, chemical peels, and body contouring, helping clients achieve smooth, radiant skin and enhanced confidence. Her holistic approach ensures that each treatment not only improves physical appearance but also promotes mental well-being, making every visit a truly transformative experience.
