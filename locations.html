Locations

Sierras

Nestled in the tranquil beauty of the Northern California Sierras, SKN+BOD Aesthetics offers a serene escape into nature’s embrace. Surrounded by towering pines, crystal-clear mountain lakes, and winding trails, the spa provides a rejuvenating experience set against a stunning backdrop of rugged landscapes and scenic vistas. The area is home to small, tight-knit communities like Truckee and Donner Lake, where the population hovers around 16,000. The pace of life here is slower, inviting visitors to relax and unwind away from the hustle and bustle of city living. Whether you’re in the mood for a rejuvenating facial, a soothing massage, or simply want to experience the natural tranquility of the mountains, SKN+BOD Aesthetics offers the perfect retreat. The vibe of SKN+BOD Aesthetics reflects the peaceful and earthy atmosphere of the Sierra region—warm, welcoming, and deeply connected to nature. Natural materials like wood, stone, and soft lighting fill the space, creating a calming environment where guests can fully immerse themselves in relaxation. The skilled aestheticians focus on holistic, sustainable treatments that are as much about mental wellness as they are about skincare, allowing visitors to leave feeling refreshed, rejuvenated, and reconnected to the beauty of the world around them.

Sacramento

In the heart of Sacramento, SKN+BOD Aesthetics offers a unique escape that combines the luxury of urban convenience with the serene wellness experience that guests rave about. This sister location provides the same exceptional quality of treatments as the Sierra retreat, but in the vibrant and dynamic atmosphere of California’s capital. Situated in a chic, modern space, the Sacramento location has quickly become a go-to destination for those seeking rejuvenation and relaxation amidst the hustle of city life. The spa’s décor mirrors the earthy, natural aesthetic of its mountain counterpart, featuring calming wood and stone accents alongside soft lighting to foster a sense of tranquility. Here, guests can enjoy the same holistic treatments, from revitalizing facials to soothing massages, with an emphasis on sustainable skincare and mental wellness. Whether you're a local or visiting the area, SKN+BOD Sacramento offers the perfect retreat to unwind and reconnect, all while embracing the spirit of California’s beauty and well-being.
